About Me!

Where I’m from

I was born and raised within the western side of Ohio in the United States, which is the epitome of Small Town, USA.

I was raised by two wonderful parents within a household of six, along with my three younger siblings. As time went on, my father would end up bringing me my love of computing.

What I do now

Currently, I am a student at Mount St. Joseph University double majoring in Computer Science as well as Web & Mobile App Development, with a minor in mathematics.

I am very involved around campus, including working as a cITe Support Scholar Co-Op, Gameday Operations Assistant, as well as an outfielder for the University baseball team.

My future plans

This coming summer, I am hoping to land my first internship in the realm of computing, preferably within a software development role.

As for the longer term, I am aspiring to become a Software Engineer or some sort of Data Scientist after University.
